Case Study

Enterprise SaaS Compliance

Automated revenue recognition for multi-year contracts, achieving 100% ASC 606 accuracy and eliminating manual spreadsheets.

The Challenge

An enterprise SaaS company with $200M+ ARR was struggling with revenue recognition compliance for their complex multi-year contracts:

  • Manual spreadsheet-based ASC 606 calculations prone to errors
  • 3-week audit preparation cycles consuming finance team resources
  • Lack of real-time visibility into deferred revenue balances
  • Inconsistent treatment of variable consideration and performance obligations
  • Compliance risk for upcoming IPO due diligence

The Solution

JINIX Solutions implemented a comprehensive revenue recognition automation platform:

ASC 606 Engine

Built automated revenue recognition logic handling complex multi-element arrangements, variable consideration, and contract modifications.

Contract Intelligence

Implemented AI-powered contract analysis to automatically identify performance obligations and revenue allocation schedules.

Audit Trail

Created comprehensive audit documentation with automatic journal entry generation and waterfall reporting.

Real-Time Dashboards

Delivered executive dashboards showing deferred revenue, unbilled revenue, and forward-looking booking projections.

The Results

100%

ASC 606 compliance accuracy with zero audit findings

90%

Reduction in month-end close time for revenue recognition

3 Days

Audit preparation time (down from 3 weeks)

$1.8M

Annual savings from eliminated consulting and manual labor costs
