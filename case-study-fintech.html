Case Study

Global Fintech Scales Billing

Unified 5 legacy billing systems into one automated engine, reducing close time by 4 days and onboarding 50,000+ users seamlessly.

The Challenge

A rapidly growing global fintech company was operating with five disparate legacy billing systems across different markets. This fragmentation caused:

  • Manual reconciliation consuming 80+ hours per month
  • Revenue leakage due to billing inconsistencies
  • 8-day month-end close cycles
  • Inability to scale operations for new markets
  • Poor customer experience with delayed invoicing

The Solution

JINIX Solutions designed and implemented a unified Quote-to-Revenue (Q2R) automation platform that:

Consolidated Systems

Migrated all 5 legacy billing systems into a single, cloud-based platform with zero revenue disruption during the transition.

Automated Workflows

Implemented end-to-end automation from quote generation to revenue recognition, eliminating manual touchpoints.

Global Scalability

Built multi-currency, multi-entity architecture supporting expansion into 12 new markets within 6 months.

Real-Time Analytics

Created unified dashboards providing instant visibility into ARR, MRR, and customer LTV across all regions.

The Results

50,000+

Users onboarded seamlessly with zero billing errors during migration

4 Days

Reduction in month-end close time (from 8 days to 4 days)

98%

Billing accuracy improvement with automated validation checks

$2.4M

Annual cost savings from operational efficiency gains
