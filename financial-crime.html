01 — What we cover

For banks, payment firms and fintechs
  • Money laundering checks. The rules and processes you use to spot money being cleaned through your business. We check they do what the regulator expects.
  • Sanctions screening. The system that checks customers and payments against sanctions lists. We make sure it catches the right names without flagging half your book.
  • Transaction monitoring. The system that flags suspicious payments. We check it is catching real risk, and not drowning your team in false alarms.
  • Fraud. Finding where you are losing money to fraud, and building the controls that stop it.
  • Investigations. When something has already gone wrong, we work out what happened and write it up so it stands up later.
  • Getting ready for an inspection. Preparing for a regulator's visit, and fixing what they found on the last one.
