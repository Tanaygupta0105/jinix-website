01 — The problem
Most billing failures are not billing failures. They are order-capture failures that took ninety days to surface.

By the time finance sees the error, the deal is signed, the service is live, and the customer has been invoiced wrong twice. The fix is never in the billing system alone — it is in the handoffs.
