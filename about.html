01 — What we are

And why

Every business runs two flows of money it cannot afford to get wrong: the one that carries revenue in, and the controls that keep illicit money out. Both are assembled from systems bought at different times, by different people. Both fail at the handoffs between them, which is where an implementation partner stops and nobody else starts.

We are independent, and it is not a positioning statement: no reseller agreement, no implementation partnership, no referral fee. That is what lets us tell a client which platform not to buy, or that the platform is fine and the problem is a process nobody owns — the two answers a firm with a licence quota cannot give.

02 — How we work

Seven commitments
  • We start with a written diagnostic. You own it whether or not you hire us.
  • We work in your stack. Nothing we build stops working when we leave.
  • We hold no reseller agreement. If the answer is the platform you already own, it costs us nothing to say so.
  • We quote a scope and a duration, not an open-ended retainer.
  • We hand over documentation before we go. A consultancy you cannot fire is a liability.
  • We tell you when the problem is not the one you asked us about.
  • We do not staff an engagement with people who learned the domain on the way to the kickoff.
